The idea of Fismonger font originally came from the need of the fish vendor for a corporate style featuring two typefaces. During the invention of some basic principles of the character, the idea of creating a modern broad font family directly intended for setting in a rich number of styles arose. First it was necessary to define the span of widths and the saturation of duct, that is the positioning of the outertmost characters in the table. The basic typeface Medium Regular was assembled sufficiently and then followed the Extra Condensed Thin, Extra Condensed Bold, Extra Extended Thin a Extra Extended Bold typefaces. In order to accomplish harmonic proportions it was necessary to define the movement on the x and y axes. reading down the column in the table, between the thinnest and the thickest typeface the dependence is left linear and equidistant. From the lightest to the boldest the dependence is slightly curved with corresponding values, so that there are smaller differences at the beginning of the spectrum in the lighter and bigger differences at the end in the bolder typefaces. By reason of preserving the original parameters in the middle of the field a projection was created and meticulously compared with the Medium Regular typeface during each move in the border positions. After creating this perfect composition the remaining characters of alphabets were constructed and according to the mentioned principle further typefaces were interpolated. The Fishmonger font is completely devoid of all flourish and ornamental shape. Constructed in a funtional, safe climate it was cleansed to the core of the character code. It may be this quality that imparts its characteristic and incommutable range of characters.
